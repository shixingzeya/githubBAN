Fidypedia

Welcome to Fidypedia. This page was designed to showcase the countries created by 7A students for their Building a Nation project. During the second semester of geography class, each group of students used their knowledge of geographical concepts to create a country of their own. A full portrait of each country includes maps, data, symbols, and stories giving detail to a wide array of physical and human geographic aspects of a nation. Each country also has its own capital city which has its own article.

The Fidypedia articles created by each group give an overview of their respective countries. This website was designed and crafted by our student Chris Chen (Chen NanYue) – big thank you to him for the drive and expertise. We hope you can use this website to enjoy the creativity, hard-work, and wit from our 7A students while also learning a bit about geography.
