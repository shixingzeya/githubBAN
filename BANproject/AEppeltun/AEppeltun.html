There are 1 national language. It is Old English. It is also known as National Lexicography Units (NLUs).

One of Æppeltún’s most well known people is Shangguan Fugui. He was its president from 100 thousands year until 95 thousands year. He died in 88 thousands year at the age of 22 thousands. The current president is Shangguan Guagua

Æppeltún is located in the eastern Himalayas, less than 200 kilometers from Mount Everest and more than 250 kilometers from the coastline, along an ocean (Pacific). The area of Æppeltún is 90,600,000 square kilometers. Æppeltún is the largest country in the world. Acsa has an altitude of 6,688 meters, which is the highest part of Æppeltún.

On the back of Æppeltún is a neighboring country blocked by a mountain, which is a country full of exotic customs.
