Epimethus

Epimethus is the capital city of Æppeltún. It is the largest city in the central Asia near by the Hima Alaya(Himalaya mountain), with around 250 thousand people in 2022.

The city is in the middle part of Æppeltún in Central Asia and is surrounded by many mountains, one lake, and many farm animals. Epimethus has an area of 8888 km2 (3555.2 sq mi). The lake Alasca is in the middle of Epimethus. The climate is temperature and wet.

Epimethus is an important city for the history of Æppeltún. The first emperor Shangguan Fugui in Æppeltún was live in this city. Because the regime has not changed, his descendants have always lived in this city, so this city is also the birthplace of this country.

Epimethus is a world city of culture and farm. Our agricultural technology ranks first in the world, and we can be found in the agricultural technology of every country. The culture in Æppeltún is mostly about agricultural technology, and even the sales of books on agriculture once became the first in the world.

Famous People In History

Shangguan Fugui

The first emperor of Æppeltún. He also saw a lot of wars when he was very young and families were torn apart, so he wanted to create a place without pain. Shangguan Fugui and Murong Goudan found a mountain and named it Æppeltún and he taught the aborigines live in the Æppeltún how to farming, weaving, raising livestock. The aborigines regarded shangguan Fugui as emperor.

Main achievements:

  • Create the Æppeltún
  • The introduction and popularization of agricultural techniques

Murong Goudan&Murong Jingxi

Murong Goudan was ShuangguanFugui’s best friend, when Shuangguan Fugui wanted to make a new country he unconditional support for Shanggua Fugui, help Shuangguan Fugui to administration and management Æppeltún.

Murong Jingxi was Murong Goudan’s thirty-fourth generation. 1286, a country found Æppeltún and they covet to the Epimethus’s agricultural technology. So they started the war but Murong Jingxi defeated them and destroyed their country. Murong Jingxi was the hero for Æppeltún. He defended himself against foreign enemies and incidentally annexed the invading country.


Sima Cuihua

A famous inventor. Sima Cuihua invent many machine and the most famous one is Perpetual motion machine for laying eggs, it take Æppeltún agriculture directly forward for 500 years.

Epimethus was very famous for agricultural. The citizens of Epimethus value food too much to waste here. Our traditional dress is the Dongbei cotton-padded jacket (worn by the first emperor Shuangguan Fugui).

Epimetheus' traditional food is manchurian stew, which almost everyone who visits Epmethus has eaten.

The most citizens in Epimethus were Ethnic Han, our country implements the policy of equality between men and women without discrimination, this policy promotes the faster development of our country.

Economy

In 2100, The GDP of epimethus ranks first in the world. The city is the largest metropolitan economy in Æppeltún. Epimethus focuses on agriculture, with 80% engaged in animal husbandry and planting, 5% engaged in forestry and 15% engaged in agricultural product research. Epimethus currently has a workforce of 3 hundred thousand.

Important economic sectors in Epimethus include life sciences, research on new agricultural products, animal husbandry, planting and Forestry.
