Little carrot

Little carrot is the capital city of Octo-Pengdom. It is the largest city in the country by population, with around 10 million people in 2010.

The city is in the central part of Octo-pengdom and is surrounded by many hills and lakes. Little carrot has an area of 1344km2.

Little carrot is home to many famous animals. As Octonauts and penguins. When you need help, the Octonauts will always protect the country out of danger.

The Octonauts are in the center of the country. They live under the lake and comes out when we need help.

History

  • 1910 Octo-pengdom was officially established in 1910 and Little carrot was established as the capital. 1914 Octonauts established.
  • 1914 Octonauts established.
  • 1915 The Octonauts is located in the capital to protect the people. This policy was officially established, called "Guardian policy".
  • 1920 The country has gradually stabilized because of the existence of the Octonauts, and the country is peaceful and the people are safe.
  • 1950 Due to global warming, Antarctic penguins moved northward to invade the capital and launch a war, known as the "Penguin war" in history.
  • 1967 The war was calmed by the submarine column and the penguins were sent back to the South Pole.
  • 1988 Captain Buck was elected president.
  • 2001 The old Octo-pod fort was knocked down due to operational failure, which made the people very panic.
  • 2015 The new Octo-pod was established under the leadership of the president, and the old Octo-pod has also become the home of giant octopus.
  • 2022 Octo-pingdom become one of the developed countries.

People

Little carrot is the most populous city in octo-pengdom.

In 2016, little carrot had 7.5million settled population and an urban area of 1344 square kilometers. The urban population density of little carrot is 179 people per square kilometer.

In 2018, the settled population of little carrot increased to 8.5 million. The city has more than 10 ethnic groups, but they all speak carrotisn. According to the urban population statistics, there are 53% married families, 10% single parent families, 29% single people and 8% teenagers.

In 2020, the population of little carrots increased by 1.5 million. Among them, 0.5 million people emigrated to the little carrot for the surrounding foreigners, and the little carrot itself increased by 1 million people.

Cuisine

The most famous food here is small fish biscuit, which is not only delicious, but also tempts all animals, which is very helpful for the follow-up work of the Octonauts.

This kind of biscuit was also brought back to the South Pole in 1967 because of the “penguin war”, and has been popular all over the world since then. The maker of this kind of biscuit, plant fish, is also known by the world and has become the world's nine Michelin genuine chefs.

Now, fish biscuit has theme stores in China, Spain and other places.

Culture

Little carrots are famous for many commercial streets, some of which have won awards. This is a fashionable city. Many talented young people set up shops in the commercial street, making little carrot a city with the most visitors in the country.
