Mulberry

Mulberry a large country in the center of the world, is also a superpower in the world, with an area of 6.5 million square kilometer, being the largest country in the world. Mulberry is a developed country with a small population of about 100,000 people. The terrain and climate of Mulberry land are complex and diverse, and the biodiversity is complete, which is the main home of a variety of wild plants and animals.

History

In 7000 BC, the ecological conditions of the area north of Mulberry were favorable for human habitation, which led to strong population growth in the area during this period. Mulberry archaeological discovery before 2000 BC. The Mulberry Basin has been inhabited by agricultural people for more than 2,000 years. This region has long been the center of civilized activity. According to excavated artifacts and oral history records, the earliest of these civilizations was a farming civilization. After the 16th century, the emergence of a rival kingdom of Bajimir and Vadai kingdom, from the three wars endless. Except for the slave raids, none of these regimes extended their control to the grasslands of the south. It was unified from 1883 to 1899.

Geography

The mulberry tree is surrounded by four countries. It has no contact with the sea, no coastline and is inland. Mulberry is the largest country in the world, with an area of 6.5 million square kilometers, about the size of China on Earth. To the west of Mulberry there is a very scary mountain range called Devil's Hill, it is the biggest mountain in the world . Occupying the entire west side of Mulberry, it is sepulchingly neglected, ecologically sound and rich in natural resources. Mulberry attached great importance to agricultural development, so they reclaimed many areas, most of which were farmland, so the terrain was flat. Of course, mulberry, as the home of the environmental protection group, is very environmentally protected, and many rare creatures live here.

Climate

Mulberry is mainly temperate. It is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean and the Indian Ocean on three sides and is located in the milder southern hemisphere. The average elevation in the equator and inland is higher. Due to the influence of the topography and oceans, Mulberry has different climates in different places.

Mulberry has a desert in the northwest, with a subtropical climate in the east, along the border and the Indian Ocean. From the east, the land quickly crossed the mountains to the back plateau, which is called the plateau. Although Mulberry is a semi-arid area, there are differences in climate and topography.

The climate in the southwest is similar to that of the Mediterranean region, with mild and humid winters and hot and dry summers. Most of Mulberry's wine is also produced in this area. This area is also famous for its wind, almost all year round. The wind is sometimes strong, which is bad for sailors, causing many ships to crash. Further east of the southern coast, the rainfall is more even throughout the year, forming a green landscape.

Economic

The main industries of Mulberry are agriculture (food crops and cash crops), handicrafts and tourism. As a country focusing on agricultural development, Mulberry's most important economic source is food cash crops. We have a wide variety of exotic varieties to satisfy almost everyone's taste, at a premium of course. We are one of the richest countries in the world because of these rare plants. Because of strange circumstances, we have almost every plant on this planet. Despite being a country of almost all farmers, we can be among the best in the world.

Population

Mulberry is a country with a population of about 100 million, with different origins, cultures, languages and religions. Mulberry has only one race, and they prohibit foreign races from occupying their place.

Province

Mulberry is divided into 16 provinces. Each province is further divided into 11 districts, 3 municipalities and 5 regional municipalities.

It is understood that Mulberry has two religions. The underworld accounted for 32%, and the Guangming accounted for 68%. Society and Culture

Some people say that because Mulberry has only one race, Mulberry people, the culture of Mulberry is relatively homogenous. Actually, mulberry has a lot of colorful culture. As an agriculture-oriented country, we can make food that satisfies all tastes, and the diversity of our food is enjoyed by all. Although the mulberry food is very popular, the food is not the only thing, we also have lots of dancing and music.

Because we are very homogeneous, our cultural traditions are the most deeply rooted. And our habits are still there, because we've just upgraded our ancestral ways of life rather than throwing them away. In addition to speaking their mother tongue, the mulberry people in the city usually speak the languages of various countries, because we have better relations with the countries of the world.

We have a lot of foreigners because we don't exclude foreigners and even want to be friendly with them. After all, mulberry people are naturally curious about everything.

Military

Mulberry has great influence in global economy, politics and military. We are the vanguard of the global peace group, representative. We are working on developing all kinds of technological and military weapons, not against other countries of course (the representatives of the Peace Group would not do such a thing), but to contribute to humanity. We have good relations with almost all countries and often help them and act as peacemakers when many international affairs arise disputes and divisions.

The Mulberry Ministry of Defense manages the armed forces, including the Army, Marines, navy and Air Force. The Coast Guard is run by the Department of Homeland Security in peacetime and the Navy in wartime. In 2008, the Mulberry Armed Force had 1.4 million active duty personnel, and hundreds of thousands each in the reserve and National Guard, for a total strength of 2.3 million.
