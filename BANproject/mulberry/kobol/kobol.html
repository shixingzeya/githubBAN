Kobol

Kobol is capital city of Mulberry, located in the middle of the Mulberry region, Kobol covers an area of about 1106 square kilometers and has a population of about 100,000. It is the largest rural development center in the Mulberry region. There is a natural lake and a man-made lake. The urban area is more developed, while the rural area has developed tourism, aquaculture and light industry.

Kobol has a temperate continental climate, located inland and at a high altitude. Summers are short and warm, and winters are long and cold, with temperatures as low as -20 degrees. Kobol is divided into four major regions: Chen town, Yu town, Liu town and A city.

Chen town

Function:

  1. To provide meat, milk, eggs and other animal food.
  2. Provide light industry with wool, cashmere, leather, bristles, animal bones, casings and other raw materials.
  3. Foreign exchange is obtained through the export of livestock products.
  4. Promote the development of animal husbandry input industry and animal product processing industry to increase employment opportunities.
  5. Provide organic fertilizers for crop production.
  6. Increase farmers' incomes.
  7. Provide animal power for agriculture and transportation. Promote the economic and cultural development of the vast pastoral areas and strengthen unity among ethnic groups.

Liu town

Function:

Liu town combines agriculture with tourism and implements agricultural tourism. Chen town gives Liu town land area and some domestic animals for tourists to visit. Agricultural tourism allows tourists to get closer to nature and adjust people, nature, economy and environment the contradiction between. In the development and construction of tourism agriculture, the main goal of Liu town tourism is not to destroy the original natural ecological environment as far as possible, reduce the artificial effect, and promote the benign cycle of agricultural ecological system.

Yu town

Function:

The village is usually processed to meet the needs of the inhabitants of Kobol. Yu's home factory provides a large number of unemployed it was difficult for people to get jobs because the Yu’s factory had a huge workload. Yu's factory was built near the forest, though there were a lot of them raw ecological material collection, but this will seriously pollute the forest environment. To solve this problem, Yu town village took relevant measures were taken.

  1. Keep polluting machines away from the ecological environment.
  2. Control heavy polluting industries.
  3. The long term the stored waste shall be disposed of by the relevant enterprises.

A city

Function:

It is a gathering place for the Commodity Exchange of surrounding farmers or handicrafts, and its commerce is mainly composed of trading markets, shops and supporting service facilities such as hotels and restaurants.

Kobol has agriculture, light industry, tourism and commerce. The versatility of Kobol shows that Mulberry has a strong ability to assemble, it facilitates the interaction between people and enterprises, and the interests of people unite us more. Sometimes benefiting different parties in the process can improve the efficiency of the distribution of goods and services.
