Ivaspa

Ivespa is located on a continent near the sea, adjacent to two countries. The country has a moderate area and an average population distribution.

The largest city in ivespa is the capital, Junjun, which is located in the center of the city. It has superior geographical conditions, many mountains and rivers, and moderate climate.

Ivespa has three national languages, Junjun, aespa and ive. Each language has a specific name, but most people in ivespa still speak Junjun.

The most famous person of ivespa is Zhuai Jie. She is a rare female president in all countries. She is also very young and promising. She has promulgated many regulations that are beneficial to the state and the people. She is now in office for 10 years.

It is rich in materials, rich in scenery, rich in artists, mild and humid climate, many plateaus, mountains, rivers and lakes, which is actually a treasure land of geomantic omen; The air is of high quality and is not polluted by external impurities. Therefore, there are many crops and wild animals.

Speaking of wild animals, ivespa is a big animal country. Due to the rich geological and natural conditions, the animal reproduction rate is high. However, ivespa is not better than many animals, but rich in species. The famous animal in China is the Black Mamba, which is the longest and fastest poisonous snake in the world and is called the death killer by people. This animal is also unique to ivespa. However, according to the artificial domestication, some wild animals are pets, so keeping all kinds of pets has also become people's pastime.

It is said that a snake demon once occupied this land. She expanded her territory in order to multiply in this fertile land. The brutal rule attracted people's hatred, so people rebelled. However, how can human beings who have no strength to bind a chicken fight against the demon clan? Therefore, at the critical moment, an external female major general Karina fought against the snake clan with her comrades in arms winter, Giselle and Ningning, and finally cut off the head of the snake demon. People supported her to sit on the throne. It is said that it was the first ruler of ivespa, a god like existence.

Plant and Wildlife

Ivespa, as a country with superior geographical conditions and material resources, is also rich in vegetation. The government issued a notice requiring strong control over the cutting of trees and the opening of wasteland, and not over development. At present, 46.88% of ivespa's land area is covered by vegetation and water. This makes ivespa rich in agricultural production, water resources and air quality index. But it also has a certain impact on the development of the city.

As an animal kingdom, ivespa has a wide variety of animals, especially snakes. Of course, ivespa's rich natural conditions provide a very good living environment for animals and do not interfere with human beings. However, some people illegally catch wild animals. The relevant departments have stipulated a treaty to catch illegal people.

Geography

Ivespa has rugged terrain, mainly plateau and mountainous areas, surrounded by many rivers, and is located near the sea in the northeast of the continent. Ivespa is a hilly area with high terrain in the northeast and low terrain in the southwest. It faces the sea behind the mountains. In the north is a hilly and mountainous area with concentrated forests. The highest peak is tiantangding at the junction of Junjun city and Huihua County in the north, with an altitude of 1210 meters; The northeast is a medium low mountain, with Huihua mountain called "City Lung"; The central part is a Hilly Basin, and the southern part is a coastal alluvial plain, which is an integral part of the Pearl River Delta.

Climate

Ivespa is adjacent to two countries and surrounded by one ocean, so it has the influence of various factors. Ivespa mainly has temperate continental climate. However, ivespa has a complex climate, with a total of eight climates.

The geological structure of Ivespa is quite complex and has good metallogenic conditions. 47 minerals have been found, with 820 mineral sites, including 22 large and medium-sized deposits. The main minerals are construction granite, cement limestone, ceramic soil, potassium, sodium feldspar, salt ore, nitrate, aspartite feldspar, fluorite, marble, mineral water and hot ore water. There is a shortage of energy minerals and non-ferrous metal minerals in the jurisdiction, with a sporadic distribution, small scale and unstable grade.
