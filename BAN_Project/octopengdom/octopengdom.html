Octo-Pengdom

Octo-Pengdom (officially called Kingdom of Penguin) is an protruded and fragmented country. The flag is white and blue stripes with a penguin in the center.

It is a large country by population, with around 200 million people in 2010. The size is also about 1,000,000 km2.

Little carrot is home to many famous animals. As Octonauts and penguins. When you need help, the Octonauts will always protect the country out of danger.

There is many city in the country and has different islands with the name by the plant fish in Octonauts.

History

1900 Organisms from the world's oceans migrated to the landdue to environmental problems, and gradually evolved into amphibians after long-term changes. Some of the land formed small islands due to sea and land migration, which were named radish.

1910 Octo-pengdom was officially established in 1910 and Littel carrot was established as the capital.

1915 The leader of the plant fish held a competition and selected several winners to become Octonauts , which is also the predecessor of Octonauts.

1914 Octonauts established.

1915 The Octonauts is located in the capital to protect the people. This policy was officially established, called "Guardian policy".

1920 The country has gradually stabilized because of the existence of the Octonauts, and the country is peaceful and the people are safe.

1950 Due to global warming, Antarctic penguins moved northward to invade the capital and launch a war, known as the "Penguin war" in history.

1958 Doctor PI in the team was doubted by the people because he was a penguin. He strongly demanded that doctor PI be replaced. Finally, the captain stopped him.

1967 The war was calmed by the submarine column and the penguins were sent back to the South Pole.

1976 A citizen found a group of left behind penguins in a cave and helped them. Octonauts knew that and provided help for them. The penguins were grateful and brought the culture, science and technology of Antarctica to Octo-pengdom, making great contributions to Octo-pengdom.

1988 Captain Buck was elected president.

2001 The old Octo-pod fort was knocked down due to operational failure, which made the people very panic.

Geography

Octo-pengdom is located at north of the whole continent and has more than 20183 kilometers long coastline. The total area of our country is 500000 km. Compared with other countries, our land area is not too large.

Little carrot has the highest mountain in our country———little carrot mountain, it’s altitude is 3080 meters. Also, there is a lake in the middle of our country, many people living nearby it .At the same time, our country does not border with other continents, and many small islands around. So our country has many good harbors.

There are many climates in our country. Such as Temperate monsoon climate, Subtropical monsoon climate, Mediterranean climate and so on. Among them, the temperate monsoon climate is the place where Octo-pengdom has a large population.

Economics

Octo-pengdom has highly developed economy, huge production scale, leading production technology and complete department structure; Agriculture and industry are developed and advanced; The traffic volume of highway and Aviation Technologies ranks first in the world; Foreign trade volume ranks second in the world; The financial industry is very developed; Its economic scale has long ranked first in the world, with a per capita GDP of more than US $20000, ranking first among countries with a population of more than 50million (inclusive). However, compared with other developed countries, Octo-pengdom is more low- key, but it pursues important economies in the world and plays an important role in human economic life.

Culture

Octo-pengdom cultural characteristics are as follows: The people of our country are gentle, warm and humorous, also good at communicating with others. At the same time, we are relatively and will not go everywhere to publicize their own property. And will be polite to talk to strangers. Another point is that there are some small shops in our country dedicated to making contributions. In other words, we will send unused things there for people in need to use. So, the people of our country are united, cooperative and help each other.

For our business, We usually don't do big business with strangers when we do some business with them. At the same time, you should arrive at the agreed place on time when trading with others, otherwise you will not continue trading with you. Because doing so will make others feel that you don't care about the deal with him.
