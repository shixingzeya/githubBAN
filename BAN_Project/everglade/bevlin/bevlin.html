Bevlin

Bevlin is the capital city of Everglade. It is the largest and most rich city in the Everglade. There are almost 4 million people live in that country. It has the largest population in the whole country.

The city is in the east-north part of Everglade and is surrounded by sea and in the city have a forest. Bevlin has an area of 1000 km2 . It is a long river begin in these city and connect the whole country, and it has a temperate climate.

Belvin is home to many weird and tripe buildings and monuments like the Queen’s House, and the National Temple. Which was the temple for a failed religion the king tried to make. Most buildings in Belvin are very bizarre, some question why Belvin is so bizarre. Belvin is definitely full of twists and turns.

Belvin is an infamous city for how unlucky they are. Belvin is also known as Disaster city for a day in 1997. Although being one of the safest cities, onFebruary, 4, 1997, a volcano on an island nearby erupted. It sent hugeTsunamis, floods, and an earthquake. Then a bunch of terrorists dropped a bomb near parliament. All this happened during The Inauguration of the president. In the end thankfully nobodyDied. But the disaster caused 1.1 billion USD in damages. And every February 4th, people mourn for the people who lost their homes and Play Lana Del Rey songs to forget the trauma. People till this day question why Belvin is the capital since it is placed at such a weird place in the north. In 2014, the country had a vote to change the capital city to Skylin ( the second biggest city in Everglade), but most people were reluctant since Belvin holds such a cultural and historic prevalence.

Population

In 2022, Bevlin had 4 million registered inhabitants in area of 890 km2 . The city's population density was 5000 inhabitants per km2. Bevlin is the most populous city in Everglade. There are more than 20 district with a population of at least 10,000 people in 2022.

In 2022, there were more than 30,000 registered residents of foreign nationality and another 3,700,000 is live Bevlin 800~900 years. When the city appear they were live here.

Culture

Bevlin is famous for its numerous cultural institutions, many of which enjoy international reputation. It is a trendsetting city. Young people, creatives and entrepreneurs continue to settle in the city and make Belvin a popular entertainment center in the world.

Economy

In 2019, the GDP of Belvin totaled to $139 billion.[1] The city is the largest metropolitan economy in Everglade and the third largest city on an island. Belvin's economy is dominated by the service sector, with around 85% of all companies doing business in services. In 2019, the total labor force in Belvin was about 2.0 million.
