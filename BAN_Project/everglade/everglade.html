Everglade

The Republic of Everglade is and island nation located in the Atlantic Ocean. It borders 9 countries including, Thromsitis,Glindia, Heiniel, Bustyrhonda, Stenhel, Alfo, Todok, Ghandars, Ihope.

The biggest city of Everglade is Belvin. The country has eight main cities for different regions. They are Belvin, Skylin,Tampas, Gonoria, New Hampshiire, Iowa,Aspridstick, Jade Isle.

There are 4 national languages. They are English, Evenese, Hungarian, and German.

English: Everglade

Evenese: Everglandes

German: Aeveganerde

Hungarian: Everglszag

One of Everglade's most well known people is George Orwell. He was a English Novelist of Everglade descent. He died in 1950 at the age of 50. He is still being praised and he is often visited by many people.

Everglade is home to many rare animals. Especially snakes.

Everglade is also one of the richest island nations in the world.

History

The first people to get the Everglade islands Were the Danish vikings. They arrived in 812 AD. Native tribes and villages were destroyed in favor of Cities built by vikings. In 1063, the War Of The Spikes Had the natives finally push all the vikings away, but the damage had been Done and white people were all over the islands.

During the 1720s to the 1810s sparked the Golden Age of Everglade. During this glorious period of time, Everglade was filled with magnificent and breathtaking art and architecture. Each major city in Everglade had Developed a style.

Belvin: “ The Capital”.

Skylin: “The City of Glass”.

Tampas: “Tropical Paradise”.

Gonoria: “Art Deco Metropolis”.

New Hampshire: “Medieval Village”.

Iowa: “The City of Industrial Realness”.

Aspridstick: “The Overgrown Jungle Garden”.

Jade Isle: Springcore Island”.

After World War I, Everglade broke into conflict with another island nation called Agathina. The Beak Peninsula wasn’t a really important place. But when miners accidentally found gold, it the main source of income. Naturally, Agathina took interest and started the Beaken Peninsula Wars. This war was one of the most deadly wars in history. Over ¼ of the Everglade population was gone.

During the U.N Security Council meeting in 1950, there was a problem with Everglade’s flag. When the flag was raised , there was a problem with the flag. The flag turned out to be very purple when in actuality it was blue. This infuriated the public and was considered a disgrace. And Everglade Refused to attend the next meeting.

Population

The entire Everglade covers 300,000 square kilometers. With a population of 14 million, it is the 8th most populous island in the world. The island with the largest population concentration is Jade Isel The largest population of Everglade is Mestizos, mainly distributed in Jade Isel. There are about 15 ethnic groups and 4 languages in Everglade. Almost everyone from all over the world has lived in Everglade, such as white, yellow, black, mixed race, etc. Everglade is called "the land of all who come". The main gathering place of white people in Everglade is North Isle and West Mainland, the main gathering place of black people in Everglade is Island, and the main gathering place of yellow people in Everglade is Southern Isel, Hybrids and various types of people or nations are mostly scattered around Jade Isel. In terms of language, Everglade has no fixed language due to its ethnic and ethnic diversity. Everglade will still have German as its official language. Everglade's native language is Evenese, and the most commonly spoken language is English.

Provinces

Everglade is divided into 8 provinces. Each province has a capital and a large city. There are 2 autonomous regions and 1 city province. There are also 6 main islands. ( East Mainland, West Mainland, Northern Isle, Southern Isle, Jade Isle ). Everglade also has 2 archipelagos. (Veniice Islands and EverMediterranean Island)

Religion

According to the 2001 national census, Christians accounted for 60% of the population (40% Roman Catholic) and (720% Protestant). Followed by Atheism at 32%, and 6% Buddhism, and 4% Islam. Overall, Everglade is not one of the most religiously diverse.
